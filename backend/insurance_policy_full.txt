Page 1, Clause 1.1: Health insurance policy covers hospitalization expenses incurred due to illness or injury.

Page 2, Clause 1.2: Coverage begins after successful payment of premium and policy activation.

Page 3, Clause 2.1: A waiting period of 30 days applies for all new policies except accidents.

Page 4, Clause 2.2: Pre-existing diseases are not covered for the first 2 years of the policy.

Page 5, Clause 3.1: Daycare procedures that do not require 24-hour hospitalization are covered.

Page 6, Clause 3.2: Outpatient consultations are not covered under this policy.

Page 7, Clause 4.1: Surgery expenses are covered up to a maximum of ₹2,00,000.

Page 8, Clause 4.2: Inpatient treatment costs including room rent and nursing are covered.

Page 9, Clause 4.3: ICU charges are covered up to ₹5,000 per day.

Page 10, Clause 5.1: Ambulance charges are reimbursed up to ₹2,000 per hospitalization.

Page 11, Clause 5.2: Emergency hospitalization must be reported within 24 hours.

Page 12, Clause 6.1: Pre-authorization is mandatory for planned hospital admissions.

Page 13, Clause 6.2: Failure to obtain pre-authorization may result in claim rejection.

Page 14, Clause 7.1: Claims exceeding ₹2,00,000 require manual verification and approval.

Page 15, Clause 7.2: Cashless claims are processed only at network hospitals.

Page 16, Clause 8.1: Cosmetic or aesthetic treatments are not covered.

Page 17, Clause 8.2: Dental treatments are excluded unless due to accidental injury.

Page 18, Clause 9.1: Mental health treatments are covered as per IRDAI guidelines.

Page 19, Clause 9.2: Maternity expenses are covered after a waiting period of 9 months.

Page 20, Clause 10.1: Newborn baby coverage starts after 90 days of birth.

Page 21, Clause 10.2: Vaccination expenses are not covered under this policy.

Page 22, Clause 11.1: Original medical bills and discharge summary must be submitted for reimbursement.

Page 23, Clause 11.2: Incomplete documentation may lead to claim rejection.

Page 24, Clause 12.1: Fraudulent claims will lead to policy termination.

Page 25, Clause 12.2: Submitting false documents will result in blacklisting of the policyholder.

Page 26, Clause 13.1: Motor insurance covers accidental damage to insured vehicles.

Page 27, Clause 13.2: Damage caused due to natural disasters is covered.

Page 28, Clause 14.1: Pre-existing vehicle damage is not covered under motor insurance.

Page 29, Clause 14.2: Driving under the influence of alcohol voids the claim.

Page 30, Clause 15.1: Third-party liability is covered up to ₹5,00,000.

Page 31, Clause 15.2: Personal accident cover is provided to the driver.

Page 32, Clause 16.1: Repair costs must be validated by authorized service centers.

Page 33, Clause 16.2: Use of non-genuine spare parts may lead to claim rejection.

Page 34, Clause 17.1: Theft claims must be reported to police within 24 hours.

Page 35, Clause 17.2: Failure to submit FIR copy will result in rejection.

Page 36, Clause 18.1: Claims involving intentional damage are not covered.

Page 37, Clause 18.2: Wear and tear damage is excluded from coverage.

Page 38, Clause 19.1: Policy renewal must be completed before expiry to maintain continuity benefits.

Page 39, Clause 19.2: Grace period of 15 days is allowed for renewal.
